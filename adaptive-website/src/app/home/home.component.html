About us

We are a student-run organization at the University of Michigan that runs modified gymnastics classes for kids with disabilities. Every week, we have 2 classes on Saturday (TIMES) and 2 classes on Sunday (10:00am - 11:00am and 11:15am - 12:15pm). Kids will have the opportunity to stretch with us, complete an obstacle course around the gym, and have free time to play! Absolutely *no* gymnastics experience is required for volunteers to participate. More information can be found in the other tabs. We hope to see you in the gym!
